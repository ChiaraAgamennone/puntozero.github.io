welcome to PUNTOZERO

PUNTOZERO is the web-lab about e-Learning, emerging ITC and manufacturing for the Post-Lauream courses at the Department of Surgical Sciences of the University of Parma, Italy.
Healthcare and Innovation are the key-words at the core of the experimental activities of PUNTOZERO web-lab capable of networking people, resources and experiences from and to different areas of edge-cutting technology and research in the field of healthcare such as Virtual Reality, e-Learning, Healthcare without harm, Computer Supported Collaborative Work, Big Data, etc...
Graduate and post-graduate education programs for health professionals and social workers might benefit from PUNTOZERO resources, dive in its innovative research programs and collaborate hands-on in its projects.
