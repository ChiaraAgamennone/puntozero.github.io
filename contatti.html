Authors and Contributors

Anybody is welcome to contribute and cooperate with our community of practice. You can @mention a GitHub username to generate a link to their profile. The resulting <a> element will link to the contributor’s GitHub Profile. For example: In 2007, Chris Wanstrath (@defunkt), PJ Hyett (@pjhyett), and Tom Preston-Werner (@mojombo) founded GitHub.
